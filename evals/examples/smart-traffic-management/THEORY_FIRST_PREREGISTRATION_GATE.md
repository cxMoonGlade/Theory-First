## Why no partial preregistration is emitted

A document containing placeholders for controller identity, incident severity,
the baseline plan, estimators, replication unit, and local validation would look
registered while leaving every choice capable of changing the result. That
would be a **false-completion illusion**, so the gate returns a status artifact
instead.
